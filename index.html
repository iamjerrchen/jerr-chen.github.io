About Me

Welcome to my journey! I am an aspiring Software Developer studying Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. Before college, I wanted to become a game developer because of my passion for gaming. After my first year in college, I learned about other career paths that intrigued me. As I continued my learnings, I learned the fundamentals of computers, common practices, and special topics. The topics I have learned includes: Operating Systems, Cyber Security, and Digital Systems. Aside from my career, I am also passionate about cooking, traveling, and lifting.

Growing up, I worked at my parents restaurant, and it was at this time that I developed a love for food. My favorite cuisines originate from Asia. I love the distinct spices used, the history and culture surrounding each dish, and especially the various takes on cooking noodles.

Traveling is another favorite of mine. Traveling allows us to immerse ourselves into the environment and learn about lifestyles of cultures different from mine. I hope you'll join me as I continue on my career, learn new recipes, visit new places, and meet my goals.

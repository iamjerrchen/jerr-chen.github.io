58 National Parks

Welcome to my lifelong journey to visit the 58 National Parks in the States! The Great Smoky Mountains was the first National Park that I visited with some friends for a Spring Break roadtrip. It was exciting getting out of the city and hiking along the trails in the nature. The views from the top of the mountain was undeniably incredible. You could tell that we were near the end of winter from the light blanket of snow from the night before and mild temperature. I didn't want the adventure to end with the Great Smoky Mountains, which is why I want to visit the other parks and unpack the wonders of nature from coast to coast. For each National Park that I visit, I want to share them here to encourage the preservation of these ecosystems for future generations. I also want to share them, so that everyone can enjoy the wonders of nature and see what I see through the lens of my camera.
